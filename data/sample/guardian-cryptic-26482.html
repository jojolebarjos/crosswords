Help

Keyboard navigation
Pressing the tab key moves the selection on to the next clue. If you hold down the shift key while you press tab, the selection will move to the previous clue. The arrow keys can be used to navigate between letters in the solution fields.
Save
You can save your answers by clicking on the save button and a cookie will be stored on your computer. Your answers will reappear next time you access the crossword.
Revert to saved
You can undo recent changes by clicking on the revert to saved button.
Clear
Click on a clue and then click on the clear button to make all the letters in the clue disappear.
Check
Highlight a completed clue then click on the check button to erase incorrect letters.
Anagram
When you get an anagram to solve, highlight the word(s) in the clue. Then click the anagram button and a pop-up window will appear. Use shuffle to help you solve the anagram and insert to enter the word into the grid.
Cheat
Click on a clue, then click on the cheat button and the solution will appear automatically. Solutions to prize crosswords will not be available before the competition's closing date.
Solution
Click on the solution button to reveal all the answers to the crossword in the grid.
Print your current solution
Click on save first. Then to go to a page with a printable version of the crossword, complete with your current solution set, click on the print version link beneath the crossword heading. NB: In your print options turn on background images and you should be able to see the grid.
